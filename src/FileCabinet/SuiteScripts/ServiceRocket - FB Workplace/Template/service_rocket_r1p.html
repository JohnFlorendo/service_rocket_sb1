What is the Rocketeer 1-Pager (R1P)?

Your time is important. The Rocketeer 1-Pager was designed to help you plan and prepare your week ahead to get the most out of your time. It contains key dates and information for the weeks ahead in a single page, reducing anxiety and allowing you to be proactive .

At ServiceRocket, we use the FranklinCovey Time Matrix to help plan your time. As Rocketeers, we seek to spend as much time in Quadrant 2 as possible, allowing us to Delight our Customers, Fellow Rocketeers, Families & Communities. See Habit 3, Put First Things First for more details.

We will continually improve the R1P to make it an essential companion for every Rocketeer. If you have any suggestions for how we can make it better, please let me know.
